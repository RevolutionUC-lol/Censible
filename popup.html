How Censible Works

Censible checks your webpages for any words or phrases that the user has previously blocked.

  • The entire Blocked Phrase must match, including spaces
  • Blocked Phrases can be any case

For example, the phrase "University of Cincinnati"

  • Blocks "University of Cincinnati Football Roster" (Star Wars matches)
  • Blocks "UNIVERSITY OF CINCINNATI" (Case does not matter)
  • Does not block "UniversityofCincinnati" (Spaces affect matching)
  • Does not block "Miami University" (All words in the phrase must be found)

Advanced Tip:
Censible supports Regular Expressions if you are comfortable using them.
